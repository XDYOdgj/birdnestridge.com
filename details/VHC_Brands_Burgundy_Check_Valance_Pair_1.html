Elegant Prairie-Style Valances for Rustic Farmhouse Decor

These prairie-style valances bring a timeless, classic country charm to your windows. With their warm burgundy checkered plaid pattern paired with soft tan tones, they effortlessly blend into rustic, vintage, or primitive cabin decor, creating a welcoming and cozy atmosphere.

Key Features

  • Two 36x36x18 inch prairie-style swag panels
  • Adjustable drawstring ties for customizable draping
  • Light filtering white cotton lining for privacy and soft light diffusion
  • Burgundy and tan plaid design for a rustic farmhouse look
  • Scalloped bottom edge for added texture and visual appeal

Why Choose These Valances?

Designed for both style and practicality, these valances offer the perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality. The white cotton lining not only helps reduce fading but also allows for subtle light filtering, making them ideal for creating a serene, private space without closing off natural light. The scalloped edge adds a touch of charm and softness to your window treatment, while the adjustable drawstring makes it easy to fine-tune the look to suit your space.
