Floral Swag Valance Curtains for a Charming, Retro Country Aesthetic

These elegant floral swag valance curtains are made from durable, non-fading polyester, ensuring they remain vibrant and long-lasting even after frequent use. The unique scalloped design adds a warm, nostalgic touch to your decor, creating a cozy and stylish atmosphere in your home. Each curtain measures 36x36 inches, featuring a 1-inch header and a 3-inch rod pocket, making them easy to install on standard or decorative rods.

Versatile Window Treatment for Every Room

Ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchens, these valance curtains can be used alone or paired with blackout curtains and additional drapes to create a layered, sophisticated look. Their light-filtering properties help reduce glare and block up to 70% of sunlight, offering both privacy and comfort without compromising on style.

Easy to Install and Maintain

With a simple rod pocket design, these curtains are easy to install and remove without the need for extra tools. Plus, they are both machine and hand washable, making cleaning a breeze. Whether you're looking to add charm, functionality, or a touch of retro style, these valance curtains are the perfect addition to your home decor.
