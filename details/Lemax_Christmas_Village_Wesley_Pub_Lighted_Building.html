Lemax Christmas Village Wesley Pub

Bring a touch of traditional English charm to your holiday decor with this beautifully crafted Lemex Christmas Village Wesley Pub. As a battery-operated collectible building, it's designed to enhance your festive display and add a nostalgic feel to your space. The lighted facade emits a warm, inviting glow that reflects the spirit of the season, creating a welcoming and magical ambiance.

Inside the pub, a detailed and realistic interior scene is carefully built to elevate the visual appeal and make it a standout addition to your Christmas village collection. The durable construction ensures that it's built to last, making it a reliable part of your holiday setup. A simple On/Off switch allows you to control the lighting with ease, giving you the flexibility to show it off whenever you like.

Whether you're decorating your mantel, windowsill, or setting up a dedicated Christmas village, this 4.5V building delivers charm and character. It requires three AA batteries (not included) and can be powered with a Lemax Power Adaptor (not included) for added convenience and control. A perfect blend of design and function, the Wesley Pub is an ideal choice for those who love holiday traditions and unique collectibles.
