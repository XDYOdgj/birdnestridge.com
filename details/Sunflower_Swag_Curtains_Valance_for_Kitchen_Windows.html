Sunflower Swag Curtains with Retro Yellow Floral Design

These sunflower swag curtains valances bring a charming and elegant touch to your space, blending style with practicality. Crafted from high-quality, lightweight fabric, they are easy to handle and maintain, making them perfect for everyday use. Their soft texture and neat stitching add a refined visual appeal that complements rustic farmhouse, retro country, and modern European interiors seamlessly.

Each curtain panel measures 28 inches in width and 36 inches in height, ensuring a harmonious fit for windows of various sizes. With a rod pocket design, they are compatible with both standard and decorative curtain rods, offering versatility in installation and style.

Whether you're updating your bedroom, kitchen, or bathroom, these valances are a great addition. They can be used alone or layered with sheer curtains and toppers to create a soft, inviting atmosphere. A perfect gift for Mother’s Day, housewarming events, or any special occasion, these sunflower swag curtains are an elegant way to enhance your home’s aesthetic while adding warmth and charm.
